~~~Ikariam 3Click~~~
Simple Chromium addon/extension.

-Automating the simplest Ikariam tasks in one click without popups.
No more "open inventory", "take a look at the highscore" or "open shop"!

-Automating and shortening (as much as possible) the watch time of Cinema Bonuses!

-Tracking all resources in all cities for free.
